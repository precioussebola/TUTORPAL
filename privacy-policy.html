Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. This policy explains how TutorPal collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

1. Information We Collect

  • Personal details (name, email, qualifications) when you sign up as a tutor or student.
  • Login credentials and basic profile information.
  • Usage data such as pages visited, booking activity, and device information.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Connect tutors with students and schools.
  • Improve our platform and user experience.
  • Send important updates or notifications about your account.

3. Data Security

We use encryption and secure servers to protect your personal information. Only authorized personnel can access sensitive data.

4. Cookies

Our website may use cookies to enhance functionality. You can choose to disable cookies in your browser settings.

5. Sharing of Information

TutorPal does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share limited data with trusted partners for operational purposes only.

6. Your Rights

  • Access or update your personal data.
  • Request deletion of your account and data.
  • Opt out of marketing communications at any time.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions, please contact: support@tutorpal.co.za
